Friendly Fire

Friendly Fire is a high-end gaming arena company founded in 2017. The first arena opened in Zagreb, Croatia. Within a short period of time the company spreads to Bosnia and Herzegovina and then to Austria. Currently, there are 5 Friendly Fire Gaming arenas, two of them in Zagreb, two in Bosnia and Herzegovina and of course one in Vienna. The company was founded by young enthusiasts who are gamers themselves and wanted to participate in the development and popularization of gaming and eSports. As a young company run by young people, Friendly Fire has a simple principle: to offer everyone an unforgettable gaming experience and fun.
